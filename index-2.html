TRANSPARENCY

No secrets

“So, here I am. I offer you my skills as a business leader… an attorney…community booster… health care advocate and long-time Democratic activist."

If elected, I promise you that my actions will be guided first and foremost by my values of openness, responsibility and fairness.

James Madison, our fourth president and a founder of the Democratic Party gravely warned us against failing to fully engage the voters in government in his remarks ABOUT: “A popular government, without popular information, or the means of acquiring it…”

Sadly, I am not convinced most Scott County residents could tell you who our supervisors are, what they do, and how much of taxpayer money they spend on what? That’s not good enough and, happily, this… I can fix.
